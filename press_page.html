 Exhibition Ice Bound is a collection of art and artists that use their own style of art to express, symbolize, and work on bringing awareness to the rapidly depleting icebergs within the poles. The process of what may be causing this decay is also shown in the exhibition as well. One of the main concerns Ben, the curator, wanted to cover throughout this exhibition is climate change and show the ways humans are directly affecting it on both sides.

 Climate change is one of the biggest issues plaguing mankind this century. One of the main problems that arise from climate change is the changes in weather causing entire ecosystems to crash and burn or freeze over. This drastic change in climate is due to the amount of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere which has been oversupplied by the burning of fossil fuels, deforestation, and animal farming over the past couple of decades.
